Amanda Liu

Amanda's main project was building a generic implementation of GraphQL Subscriptions, a much-awaited technology in the GraphQL community. She investigated possible approaches, developed an execution framework for the server, and designed and implemented a Websocket transport to enable clients to subscribe to data and receive results. Additionally, Amanda helped integrate Apollo technology into Meteor Galaxy, our production hosting platform for Meteor apps, and worked on bug fixes and improvements to Apollo Client.
